Blue Infrastructure


Blue Infrastructure is a way to view the water resources around us to see how it best contributes to our needs for preservation of ancient water sources, ecological soundness and healthy drinking water.

Why Utility Map Initiative?

Utility maps are important any time you are breaking ground as they show accurate
positions of burried utitilies you will encounter. It also helps to prevent digging into or
damaging any utitilies that may cause harm to public or your workforce.
